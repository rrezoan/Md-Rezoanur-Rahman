About

I’m a doctoral researcher at the University of Wollongong (2022–2026), working on phonon/electronic engineering of carbon-modified Mg₂Si to achieve ultra-low lattice thermal conductivity. I combine solid-state synthesis, multi-instrument characterisation, and reproducible analysis to build thermoelectric and polymer-based materials that move heat and charge the way we want.
